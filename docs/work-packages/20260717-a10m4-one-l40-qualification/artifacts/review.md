# A10M4 review

Disposition: accept.

The final evidence answers the frozen implementation question without
broadening the scientific claim. The real accepted corpus can be verified and
loaded role-safely under the canonical Lemhi stack; the frozen model can make a
finite L40 update; checkpoint/restart is exact; Philox generation is nested,
order-independent, Gregorian, and supported; and the portable CPU export and
representative benchmark protocol execute completely with bounded resources.

The ten amendments are substantive audit evidence, not hidden retries. They
localized environment packaging, deterministic-runtime, job-local cleanup,
masked-data, cursor, RNG-placement, and faithful-output predicates before any
scored output existed. Amendment 009's 365-day hypothesis was explicitly
falsified by run 10 and corrected prospectively in amendment 010 against the
byte-pinned faithful fixture. No amendment changed the model family, grid,
roles, optimizer, thresholds, allocation shape, or selector contract.

The diagnostic runtime ratios would mostly fall in the later selector's FAIL
range, but interpreting them here would be invalid: the M4 network received
only two restart-qualification steps, the timing export is not a trained
candidate, and the package explicitly forbids classification. A10M5 must train
and evaluate its own exact candidate identities under the frozen contract.

No protected development or confirmation target series was accessed, and no
fitted artifact survived cleanup. The package used half its requested ceiling,
all remote roots are absent, and the successor can proceed without a scientific
or operational contract change.
