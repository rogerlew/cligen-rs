# A10M1 third-party data notices

## Daymet V4 R1

Daymet V4 R1 is distributed by NASA's ORNL DAAC as dataset DOI
`10.3334/ORNLDAAC/2129`. A10M1 retains source identity, access date, query,
hash, and the required scientific citation in its manifests. NASA Earth
science data are openly available; NASA-led unmarked datasets are made
available under CC0 under the Earthdata data-use policy. The repository does
not relicense the source data. Routine raw and normalized training bytes stay
outside Git.

## NOAA USCRN

USCRN products are distributed publicly by NOAA NCEI. Federal environmental
data hosted by NCEI are public domain/CC0 unless specifically marked
otherwise. A10M1 retains product, format, station-year URL, access date, and
hash and follows the product page's requested citation to Diamond et al. The
repository does not imply NOAA endorsement and does not relicense the data.

## Optional products

PRISM data are copyrighted and carry attribution/access-date conditions.
Because the primary Daymet/USCRN design can answer M1's question, PRISM bytes
are not acquired or redistributed. gridMET is likewise not acquired in M1;
its licensing/product identity would require a fresh recorded adjudication
before retention.
