## Objective

Correct the two bounded defects exposed by A9c3 before considering another
successor-family candidate: make the mandatory comparison surface honest and
auditable by source, and generate storm-event solar radiation, wind speed,
and relative humidity from distributions with physical support. Refit and
rerun the unchanged A9c3 development comparison under a fresh campaign
identity, without adding a selector, a corpus search, or runtime behavior.

## Scope

Included:

- exact verification of the accepted A9c3 package and report identities;
- a pre-output audit decomposing mandatory objective availability into
  observed, faithful, and historical A9c3-candidate causes;
- a candidate-blind, prospective common-support mask determined only from
  observed and faithful availability, with excluded cells retained as
  explicit report-only evidence;
- fresh fits for the same eight registered configurations, with only the
  event-context laws changed: solar radiation and wind speed use log support,
  relative humidity uses logit support, and air temperature remains unbounded;
- the same stations, roles, grouped storm estimator, thresholds, burns,
  horizons, stage limits, degradation rule, and lexicographic selection rule
  as A9c3;
- one sealed research candidate at most, a public report, independent internal
  reviews, and one registered terminal.

Excluded:

- changing A9c3 evidence, hiding an unavailable cell, assigning a favorable
  score to unavailable evidence, or using A9c4 candidate output to define the
  evaluation mask;
- output clipping, repair, fallback, arbitrary support floors, station or
  corpus expansion, selector changes, threshold relaxation, or new model
  mechanisms outside the three event-context marginals;
- access to locked confirmation station-year series, A9d execution, production
  Rust profiles, openWEPP, or WEPPcloud integration.
