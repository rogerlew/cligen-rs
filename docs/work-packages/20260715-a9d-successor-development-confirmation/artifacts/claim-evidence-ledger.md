## Residual uncertainty

- The observed-development comparison is bounded to its 20 Daymet daily sites,
  12 USCRN storm-role objects, six frozen strata, 2010--2025 period, and the
  accepted A9c4 surface.
- Hot-arid storm descriptors use only Yuma and Stovepipe Wells with equal site
  weight; they do not establish spatial universality.
- Nineteen originally applicable cells remain report-only, including missing
  wet-amount and compound-context coverage in sparse dry regimes.
- The positive-amount calibration intentionally leaves fitted occurrence
  frequencies unchanged; observed zero-wet months expose a model-support
  limitation.
- No locked 18-site confirmation outcome exists, so development improvements
  are not independent confirmation evidence.
- Multiple inherited descriptive fields in the hash-bound runtime record are
  stale; the design freeze and executed artifacts govern the actual 18/4/2
  funnel.
