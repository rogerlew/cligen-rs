and Cargo vendor archive all passed before staging. The final Cargo-vendor

inspection showed the supervisor asset was not executable. Its exact marked
remote root was removed and its private controller receipts were retained. The
same immutable plan was staged again after correcting only the executable mode;
all eleven byte/hash identities and all three executable modes then passed.

failure receipt localize the stop to the compound entry guard requiring

least one prohibited ambient variable was present despite `--export=NONE`.
The job stopped before creating the job-local base, importing Python, or
accessing candidate/development/confirmation data.
