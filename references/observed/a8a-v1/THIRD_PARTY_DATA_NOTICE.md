Snapshot and access date: **2026-07-15**

This directory contains small immutable extracts from Daymet V4 R1 and
GHCN-Daily plus the official GHCN station list used for identity verification.

these externally maintained data.

## Daymet V4 R1

The Daymet files are official Single Pixel Extraction Web Services responses
for the exact frozen coordinates and `prcp`, `tmax`, and `tmin` variables.
NASA Earthdata identifies Version 4.1, DOI `10.3334/ORNLDAAC/2129`, an extent
through 2025-12-31, and open sharing without restriction under EOSDIS data-use
and citation guidance.

Citation:

> Thornton, M. M., R. Shrestha, Y. Wei, P. E. Thornton, and S-C. Kao. 2022.
> *Daymet: Daily Surface Weather Data on a 1-km Grid for North America,
> Version 4 R1*. Version 4.1. ORNL Distributed Active Archive Center.
> <https://doi.org/10.3334/ORNLDAAC/2129>. Accessed 2026-07-15.

Official sources reviewed 2026-07-15:

- <https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov/data/catalog/ornl-cloud-daymet-daily-v4r1-2129-4.1>
- <https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov/engage/open-data-services-software-policies/data-use-guidance>

## GHCN-Daily U.S. Cooperative records

The GHCN files are deliberately limited to identifiers beginning `USC`: U.S.
records from the Cooperative Network. The official GHCN documentation defines
the country/network code and warns that restrictions can apply to contributed
non-U.S. records; those records are outside this archive. The project makes no
blanket licensing claim about the global composite.

Citation:

> Menne, M. J., et al. 2012. *Global Historical Climatology Network - Daily
> (GHCN-Daily), Version 3* [A8a U.S. Cooperative subset]. NOAA National
> Climatic Data Center. <https://doi.org/10.7289/V5D21VHZ>. Accessed
> 2026-07-15.

Official sources reviewed 2026-07-15:

- <https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/land-based-station/global-historical-climatology-network-daily>
- <https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/pub/data/ghcn/daily/readme.txt>
